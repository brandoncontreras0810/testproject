This week's movie spotlight:

Sanjuro(1963) directed by Akira Kurosawa

The first japanese film to be featured on the spotlight! Toshiro Mifune's ronin returns in this sequel to Yojimbo(another nasty movie) to create an experience unlike any other. The super ronin returns as a band of other samurai are put into a difficult position as corrpution is shown in the superintendent Kikui trying to remove the chamberlain from power. Almost setup to die and regretful of their actions, the band of samurai are saved by the quick thinking of Mifune's Sanjuro as he plays an aloof, but deep harbinger of justice as he joins the samurai to save the chamberlain from superintendent Kikui. Sanjuro is shown to be more than above the normal level of an average samurai as he can cut his way thorugh anyone or anything with his sword, as he's able to take out a small army with his sword and his skills.
